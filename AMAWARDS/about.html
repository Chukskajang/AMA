UNIQUE

AMAWARDS is a unique award ceremony aimed at recognizing and honouring exceptional private individuals, companies and reputable public servants and private businesses. This people must have over the years been able to establish positive impact on the socio-economic development of the country taking Abuja the capital as the point of focus.

ABUSE OF VULNERABLE WOMEN & CHILDREN

This year our drive is channelled towards vulnerable women & children especially those subjected to abuse as a result of several human and non-human factors like Boko Haram insurgency, Fulani herdsmen menace, flooding etc.

We aim to expose the scourge of abuse on vulnerable women and children especially those exposed to degrading factors like rape, human trafficking, extortion and many more. We hope to enlighten the public on dangers, implications and preventive measures and also use this platform to support and encourage those that are victims of this abnormal and inhuman menace. We hereby use this as a wakeup call to Nigerians; we must fight and stand against it.
